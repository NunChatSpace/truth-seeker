# Benchmark v9 Claim

## Claim

Truth Seeker `focused` reaches decisive falsifying evidence efficiently and derives a replacement hypothesis from that observation without requiring the model to narrate every post-falsification probe.

## Corrections from v8

- The required post-falsification probe audit and replacement-basis field are removed from the treatment schema.
- The focused policy returns to a compact falsify, replace, verify, and stop protocol.
- Objective classification, necessary post-false probes, unjustified continuation, retries, broad search, and distractor exposure remain offline trace telemetry rather than prompt obligations or automatic policy failures.
- v8 causes, evidence, uncertainty, and the rollback decision are recorded in `benchmarks/findings-v8.md`.
- Raw v0-v8 traces and scores remain unchanged.

## Primary Thresholds

- Focused correctness, valid behavioral verification, and falsification audit completeness are 100% at every level.
- At high complexity, focused commands and tool-output proxy to falsification each decrease by at least 30% relative to baseline.
- Total model tokens and all post-falsification telemetry are reported separately.

## Calibration Gate

Deterministic scoring must pass before paid execution. One repetition per cell is calibration only; repeated evidence is required before a public efficacy claim.

## Non-claims

The benchmark does not infer model-token usage at the falsification point, prove independent hypothesis generation, or generalize from one repetition.
