## Focused level

Use the minimum evidence sufficient for a low-risk decision. Keep classification mostly implicit when it is obvious, but surface every blocking unknown, material assumption, retry reason, approval boundary, and verification result.

Investigation budget: one visible hypothesis record, one targeted probe, then one decision checkpoint. A second probe is allowed only when the first result is inconclusive and a named unknown blocks the decision. Do not spend tools checking working directory, listing files, permissions, or other preconditions unless observed evidence makes that check necessary.
